INTERNATIONAL FORUM “EDUCATION, SCIENCE, INNOVATIONS: HUMAN CAPITAL IN THE POST-WAR REBUILDING OF UKRAINE”

11–12 December 2025

Mykhailo Drahomanov Ukrainian State University, Kyiv

Working languages: Ukrainian, English (with simultaneous interpretation).

On 11–12 December 2025, the International Forum “Education, Science, Innovations: Human Capital in the Post-War Rebuilding of Ukraine” will take place at Mykhailo Drahomanov Ukrainian State University.

The Forum will serve as a platform to unite Ukrainian and international scholars, educators, government representatives, international organizations, and civil society to form a strategic vision for the development of human capital as a key resource for Ukraine’s post-war innovative transformation.

Mission of the Forum

The Forum aims to bring together educators, scientists, Ukrainian and international civil organizations, policymakers, representatives of governmental and non-governmental institutions, and international experts to develop strategies for advancing science and education for Ukraine’s renewal and innovative transformation in the context of achieving sustainable peace.

Format

The Forum will be held in a hybrid format (in-person / online) and will include:

  • Plenary sessions,
  • Panel discussions,
  • Thematic sections,
  • Round tables,
  • Special workshops.

Expected Results

  • Preparation of proposals to strengthen the role of education and science in developing human capital in the post-war period;
  • Development of recommendations for modernizing educational programs and teacher training systems;
  • Formulation of proposals to expand international educational and scientific cooperation;
  • Consolidation of the expert community for Ukraine’s effective reconstruction;
  • Preparation of resolutions and appeals to state authorities and the academic community.
